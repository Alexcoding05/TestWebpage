About Us

We strive to ensure every dog feels just as happy boarding with us as they do at home. Throughout the day, we take them on walks and spend time playing with them. When the weather is nice, dogs enjoy the outdoors for most of the day. If the weather doesn't cooperate, we make sure they still get out at least three times a day for an hour each. You're welcome to bring your pet's food, bedding, toys, and other familiar items to make their stay more comfortable. We groom all breeds of dogs and offer a variety of shampoos and flea/tick treatments, including whitening, organic options, flea shampoos, dips, and more. The products we use help condition and leave your dog's coat shiny and smelling wonderful. Every groom or bath is finished with a colorful bandana—because your pet deserves to look and feel their best!
